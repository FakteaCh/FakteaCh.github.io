OUR MISSION.

Four AIMS of UESI (UESI THILTUM PALI TE)

1. Evangelism : Kan zirlaipuite hnenah Lal Isua Krista’n ama nihna a insawi angte chu hrilh ve a Lal Isua Krista chu anmahni mimal nunah Lal leh Chhandamtu leh Pathian ah an lo pawm ve a nun thar an lo neih theih nan.
2. Fellowship : Kan rin ang ring ve kan zirlaipuite nen a inpawlho thin a, kan lo intihchaktawn theih nan a bik takin Bible zirho leh tawngtaiho te neih thin a, Krista thuhretu ni tura infuih thin.
3. Testimony : Kan zirna hmunah kan nun chu testimony, Kristian zirtirna thudik ropui tak phochhuaktu a lo nih a, mihring nun pumpui leh harsatna zawng zawng sutkian nan a thiltihtheihna pholan.
4. Mission : Pathianin mission hna thawk tura thupek min pek hi a pawimawhzia zirlaite zinga hriattir a, khawvel pum hi Pathian ram a lo nih theih nan anni pawhin a thu chu zawm ve tura fuih.

Uniqueness of UESI (UESI danglam bikna)

UESI is Bible-believing (Bible Ringtu)
UESI is Evangelical
UESI is Evangelistic
UESI is a Fellowship of New Life (Nun Thar Nei Inpawlkhawm)
UESI is International in its Sympathies (Ram danga thawkte nen kan inhrethiam tawn)

UESI is Interdenominational (Kohhran pawl hrang hrang kan leng vek)

UESI is Spiritually Centered (Thlarau lam kan ngaipawimawh ber)
UESI is Financially Independent (sum mahniin kan intumchawp)
UESI is an Indian Organization (India ram chhulchhuak kan ni)
UESI is neither a Church nor a Sect (Kohhran emaw kalsual pawl emaw kan ni lo)
